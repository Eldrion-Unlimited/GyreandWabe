The Registry

The Gyre & Wabe Registry is the official archive of authenticated artifacts issued by the house. Each entry records a distinct work by registry designation, SKU, realm, edition status, and archival note. To be entered into the Registry is to be recognized as an authentic original of Gyre & Wabe.

Registry Charter

The Registry exists to identify, preserve, and authenticate the issued works of Gyre & Wabe. Every recorded entry stands as part of the permanent house archive and serves as a recognized document of origin, designation, and authorship.

Archive Standard

Entries are organized by registry number, mapped to SKU, and preserved as official records of the house. This archive distinguishes authentic originals, reinforces provenance, and establishes continuity across future releases.
