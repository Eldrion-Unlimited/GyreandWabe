The Registry

The Gyre & Wabe Registry serves as the official archive of authenticated artifacts issued by the house. Each entry records a distinct work by registry designation, SKU, realm, edition status, and archival note. To be entered into the Registry is to be recognized as an authentic original of Gyre & Wabe.
